9/11 terrorist network

Data visualizes the network of contacts amount hijackers and related terrorists involved in the September 2001 attack in the US.

Hypothesis

The network of terrorists is intermingled enough that removal of multiple people would likely not have enough of a disruptive effect to halt the operations

*NOTE* Can remove nodes by pressing on them, to reset refresh page

source - V. Krebs. "Mapping Networks of Terrorist Cells".
