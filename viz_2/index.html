To fully understand this visual it's important to have some basic understanding of poker rules and terminology. At the start of each round each player is dealt two cards that only they can see, two card hands are represented by their abreviated name Ace - A, Queen - Q etc. These sets of cards can be either suited, two of the same kind, or offsuited, which are denoted in the graphics as s or o respectively.An example of a valid hand could be KQs, which represents King-Queen where they both share the same suit. In poker preflop simply means the round of betting before any additional cards are laid out. Your chance of winning displayed below is your preflop win percentage which means with a given hand and no further action what is the likelihood of winning after the 5 cards are dealt.
